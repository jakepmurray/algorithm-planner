How this works

USA Ultimate's real Algorithm iterates every team's full schedule to convergence, so it can't be reproduced exactly client-side. This simulator uses the documented per-game formula (game_PR = opponent_rating ± 400 / max(2/3, 2.5 × (losing_score / winning_score)²)) and blends new games into each team's current rating as a simple weighted average against their games played so far. Treat results as directional estimates, not official projections — always cross-check against the official rankings once they update.
